Candied Walnuts

Candied walnuts sound like a big under taking but these sweet treats can be made in less then 10 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup walnuts
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tablespoon butter
  • 1/2 teaspoon cinnamon

Steps

  1. Add ingredients to a saucepan and heat to medium high
  2. Stir constantly for 5 minutes careful not to let it burn
  3. Remove from heat and put on parchment paper
  4. Allow to cool for 5 minutes
